Places to Visit in Agra

  • Taj Mahal - Marvel at one of the Seven Wonders of the World, the iconic monument of love
  • Agra Fort - Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site and Mughal architectural masterpiece
  • Mehtab Bagh - Enjoy sunset views of Taj Mahal from across the Yamuna River (optional)
  • Itmad-ud-Daulah (Baby Taj) - Visit the beautiful marble tomb (optional, if time permits)
  • Local Markets - Shop for marble handicrafts and souvenirs (optional)

Tour Duration & Itinerary

This is a same-day tour (14-16 hours) covering approximately 600 km round trip via Agra-Lucknow Expressway. Very early morning departure is essential for this long-distance tour.

Typical Schedule:

  • 3:00 AM - 4:00 AM: Pickup from your location in Lucknow
  • 7:00 AM - 7:30 AM: Arrive in Agra, breakfast break (optional)
  • 8:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Visit Taj Mahal (opens at sunrise)
  • 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM: Visit Agra Fort
  • 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Lunch break (at your own expense)
  • 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM: Optional: Visit Baby Taj or Mehtab Bagh / Shopping
  • 3:30 PM - 4:00 PM: Depart from Agra
  • 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM: Drop back to your location in Lucknow

Important: Taj Mahal is closed on Fridays. Plan your visit accordingly.

Best Time: October to March for pleasant weather. Avoid summer months (April-June).

Tips & Recommendations

  • Book tickets online in advance at www.asiagracircle.in to avoid long queues
  • Carry valid ID proof (Aadhaar/Passport/Voter ID) for monument entry
  • Wear comfortable shoes as there's extensive walking involved
  • Carry sunscreen, sunglasses, and hat during summer months
  • Photography is allowed outside but not inside the main mausoleum
  • Tripods, food items, and large bags are not allowed inside Taj Mahal
  • Hire an authorized guide at the monument for better understanding (‚Çπ200-500)
  • Start very early (3-4 AM) to make the most of your day trip
  • Keep some cash handy for entry fees and small purchases
