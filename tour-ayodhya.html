Places to Visit in Ayodhya

  • Ram Janmabhoomi Temple - Visit the sacred birthplace of Lord Rama (new temple complex)
  • Hanuman Garhi - Ancient temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman on a hilltop
  • Kanak Bhawan - Beautiful temple gifted to Goddess Sita
  • Nageshwarnath Temple - One of the oldest Shiva temples in Ayodhya
  • Ram Ki Paidi - Sacred ghats on the banks of Saryu River
  • Dashrath Mahal - Palace of King Dashrath (optional)
  • Tulsi Smarak Bhawan - Museum dedicated to Tulsidas (optional)
  • Saryu River Aarti - Evening aarti ceremony (if time permits)

Tour Duration & Itinerary

This is a same-day tour (12-14 hours) covering approximately 260 km round trip. Early morning departure is recommended to make the most of your day.

Typical Schedule:

  • 5:00 AM - 6:00 AM: Pickup from your location in Lucknow
  • 8:00 AM - 8:30 AM: Arrive in Ayodhya, start temple visits
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch break (at your own expense)
  • 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Continue visiting remaining temples and ghats
  • 4:00 PM - 4:30 PM: Depart from Ayodhya
  • 6:30 PM - 7:30 PM: Drop back to your location in Lucknow

Note: Timings are approximate and may vary based on traffic, temple crowds, and your preferences.

Tips & Recommendations

  • Start early (5-6 AM) to avoid crowds at Ram Janmabhoomi Temple
  • Dress modestly as you'll be visiting religious sites
  • Carry valid ID proof for temple entry (Aadhaar/Voter ID/Driving License)
  • Mobile phones and cameras may not be allowed inside some temples
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes as there's considerable walking involved
  • Carry water bottles and light snacks for the journey
  • Book in advance during peak seasons and festivals
