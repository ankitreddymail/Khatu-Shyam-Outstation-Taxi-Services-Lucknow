Places to Visit in Nainital

  • Naini Lake - Enjoy boating on the beautiful pear-shaped lake surrounded by hills
  • Naina Devi Temple - Visit the sacred temple on the northern shore of Naini Lake
  • Snow View Point - Get panoramic views of Himalayan peaks via cable car
  • Mall Road - Stroll along the vibrant shopping and dining street
  • Tiffin Top (Dorothy's Seat) - Trek to this scenic viewpoint for stunning vistas
  • Eco Cave Gardens - Explore interconnected natural caves and hanging gardens
  • Nainital Zoo - See rare Himalayan wildlife including snow leopards
  • Kilbury Bird Sanctuary - Perfect for bird watching enthusiasts (optional)
  • Bhimtal / Sattal - Visit nearby serene lakes (optional, Day 2)

3 Days Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Lucknow to Nainital (350 km, 8-9 hours)

  • 6:00 AM: Pickup from your location in Lucknow
  • 12:00 PM: Lunch break en route (Bareilly/Haldwani)
  • 2:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Arrive in Nainital, check-in to hotel
  • 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM: Evening walk at Mall Road and Naini Lake
  • 7:00 PM onwards: Dinner and overnight stay at hotel

Day 2: Nainital Local Sightseeing

  • 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM: Breakfast at hotel
  • 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Visit Snow View Point, Naina Devi Temple, Tiffin Top
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch break
  • 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Visit Eco Cave Gardens, Nainital Zoo, or nearby Bhimtal/Sattal
  • 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM: Boating at Naini Lake, shopping at Mall Road
  • 7:00 PM onwards: Dinner and overnight stay at hotel

Day 3: Nainital to Lucknow (350 km, 8-9 hours)

  • 7:00 AM - 8:00 AM: Breakfast and hotel checkout
  • 8:00 AM: Depart from Nainital
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch break en route
  • 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Arrive in Lucknow, drop at your location

Note: Itinerary is flexible and can be customized based on your preferences and weather conditions.

Tips & Recommendations

  • Best Time to Visit: March to June (summer) and September to November (autumn)
  • Winter Visit: December to February can be very cold with possible snowfall - carry heavy woolens
  • Book hotels in advance, especially during peak season (May-June)
  • Carry warm clothes even in summer as evenings can be chilly
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes for hill station exploration
  • Keep motion sickness medication handy for hilly roads
  • Carry valid ID proof for hotel check-in
  • ATMs are available but carry sufficient cash for small expenses
  • Mobile network can be patchy in some areas
